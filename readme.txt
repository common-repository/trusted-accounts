== Description ==
Stop harmful users from creating multiple accounts and keep your community authentic. 

Are you tired of moderating bots and abusive users on your platform? Check out Trusted Accounts. It takes care of all the complex validation stuff to ensure your users are real and trustworthy, so you can focus on growing your platform.

= Abusive users & bots cost you time, money and nerves =

- Abusive users and bots create lots of accounts, making your platform less trustworthy.

- Real users loose trust in your platform, causing them to leave or be less active.

- It takes a lot of time and money to find and remove abusive users.

- Bots and abusive users fill your platform with harmful content, making it less enjoyable for everyone else.

= Get all the tools you need to get abusive users and bots under control. =

* Background validation (no user interaction required, for minimal friction)
* Human verification (get trustworthy users, prevent fake accounts)
* Adaptive security levels & fully flexibile integration
* Block/unblock verified humans
* Custom Branding (set your brand color and logo)
* Setup Wizard for easy install
* Compatible with most plugins and themes
